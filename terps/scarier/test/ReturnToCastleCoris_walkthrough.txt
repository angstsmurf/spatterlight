#     (Location43, ONE level above the waterfall cave), not the Earth Tunnel two

#     yields the sack +5, and `get eaters` then auto-catches them into it +5).
#   * Gold ring vs. the alehouse: the doc keeps the gold ring worn from the cave
#     nest right through to Christiana, but entering the main alehouse
#     (Location93) while wearing/carrying gold triggers the "the men see the gold
#     ... and they attack you" instant-death.  Remove and bag the ring before the
#     first alehouse entry ("remove ring / put ring in bag"); at Christiana the
#     doc's "remove ring" (it is no longer worn) becomes "get ring" (from the
#     bag) before "give ring".
#   * Flambeau's house: the doc's single "s" stops at the north-end RED-door
#     house.  Flambeau lives behind the GREEN door halfway along South Lane
#     (Location91); one extra "s" reaches it so "pick lock" (needs the wire +5)
#     works.
