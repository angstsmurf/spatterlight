Title:  SIX SILVER BULLETS

/////SIX///////////////////
///////SILVER//////////////
//////////BULLETS///////////

A Game of Mystery by William Dooling
(Type HELP or CREDITS at any time)

===========================



You are asleep, and sleep is a terrible thing.
You must WAKE UP!


Dead or Dreaming?

You cannot LOOK here, for there is nothing to see.
You are dead, or dreaming. Perhaps both.
There is nothing to do except WAKE UP
...or is there?

> wake up

"Who dares insult us with this blasphemous mockery? Seize him and unmask him --that we may know whom we have to hang at sunrise, from the battlements!"

- Edgar Allen Poe, the Mask of Red Death

***

You dream of a woman dressed in green. Beautiful. Vivacious. Her eyes captivate you. She speaks of faraway lands and secret plans. You cannot help but listen. You have a part to play in her latest scheme. You must remember.

She pulls you close, and whispers something in your ear. The word barely escapes her perfect lips when a shot rings out. Blood. Madness. This is a nightmare.

You awaken.

You know nothing about yourself. If you had memories, they are gone.

Before you have a chance to LOOK, or cry out for HELP, you hear something. The voice in your head. It tells you things. Details. Dangers. Things other people would miss. Life is a game. You are a talented player. You are the Silver Agent.



> look
The Suite

Woke up here. Seems expensive. Wallpaper's terrible. Silver and gold. Floor is tile. Well maintained. 

There is a bed. A feeling of horror. Do not sleep. Must not sleep.
There is a door. Opens to the Southeast. There is a peephole. 
There is a window. Opens to the Northwest.
There is a safe. Do you know the combination?
There is a table. Laquered. Beautiful. 

On the table: A yellow note, a silver gun, and six silver bullets


> examine table

Exceptional woodwork. Tectona grandis. Sri Lankan Teak. Very Expensive.  The Yellow Note, The Silver Gun and The Silver Bullets  


> examine note

Paper is a dull yellow. They call this ivory? Faint watermark. A serpent eating its own tail. Orobos. Infinity and extinction. Who sent this? You must find them.  


> take note
You take the Yellow Note from the Table.
(from you)



> take gun
You take the Silver Gun from the Table.
(from you)



> inventory
You have The Yellow Note and The Silver Gun.  
And zero Silver Bullets


> examine peephole

Small, dark, like an eye into hell. The hall beyond opens onto the lobby.  

The purple agent waits in the hall...  


> drop note
You put down the Yellow Note.


> inventory
You have The Silver Gun.  
And zero Silver Bullets
