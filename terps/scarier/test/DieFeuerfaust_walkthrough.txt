# Die Feuerfaust -- The Fist of Fire -- ADRIFT 5 walkthrough regression script.
#
# Source: the game's OWN in-game WALKTHROUGH command (task 'wlkthrgh'), which is
# port-accurate (correct verbs, the KATZY dial-cheat), unlike the old Spectrum
# CASA solution.  Adapted here to route around the handful of commands the ADRIFT
# 5 parser rejects and the item-timing traps the author's list mis-orders.
#
# MAXIMUM SCORE: reaches the Score>=800 "scoring the maximum 800 points!" banner
# (Endgame11; internal 804 -- the author awards >800 total, exactly-800 is not a
# reachable sum).  Beyond the built-in solution's 791, this script adds: `listen
# to traders` (+2 Task146), `search beams` at the Lubeck cathedral porch (+3
# Task380, independent of `look under beams`), `look around corner of tent`
# behind the storage tent (+3 LookAround8), and the SPLIT casts at the barricade
# -- heile/stark at militiamen and captain separately (Task367+Task369+
# CastHeileA5+CastHeileA7 = +20) instead of `at all` (Task370+CastHeileA8 = +10).
# The split costs 2 extra turns inside the Barbarians4 attack window (15 turns
# from the attack to KilledByNa death, disarmed only by reaching the burning
# house, Location48), so the two optional square examines `climb onto barricade`
# and `x wagon` are dropped to restore the margin.  NOT obtainable (verified
# author bugs, FD ground-truth agrees): `x cottages` (+3, the cottages are Part
# of the Hamlet object but invisible in both engines), `ask willy about the
# men's plot` (+3 Task82, shadowed by the higher-priority AskLandlor1 catch-all),
# `x street` at the SE corner (+3 Task415, unresolvable "street" ambiguity),
# `show amulet to gundred` (+5 ShowAmulet3, general show preempts in both
# engines), `look out of slit` in the red tent (+3 LookOutOfS1, no executor).
# The chieftain's-tent slit (+5 CTentSlitS) is a trap: the chieftain spots it on
# your exit and you are captured (forfeits ReturnOuts +5 and the game).
#
