   Treasure Hunt in the Amazon

Among your late uncle's documents you find a letter from one of his friends, who used to be quite an adventurer.  In the letter it is mentioned that there is a treasure, a magnificent jeweled crown, somewhere in a mysterious temple in the middle of the Amazon. The letter contains latitude and longitude and so, you begin to pack your most important objects needed for a treasure hunt in the Amazon.

On board the airplane, you read a newspaper, and you find, that the area you are about to visit will be flooded within ten days, when a new dam is being opened.

When the airplane lands and the pilot is about to leave you in the middle of the Amazon, he promises to pick you up again on the 10th of July twelve o'clock.  He also tells you that there is a trading post nearby, where you can buy what you need. Then he takes off...
