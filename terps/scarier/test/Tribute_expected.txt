You are comfortably seated in the very first carriage of the train going north to Valodsci, your hometown. Soon you will pass by the mysterious city where you experienced so much adventure. Right now it feels like a dream. But the letter from Evaine proves that everything was real. Once again you unfold the letter and read it:

"Thank you for everything you have done.  More people are in your debt than will ever know it.  But you did not seem to be getting better in the City; even with the Crystal broken and the shards buried, it seemed that it was perhaps exerting a power over you.  As you asked, I've sent you on on the journey you meant to make at the start. I hope you find peace and a happy future; if you find yourself at loose ends after your other obligations are complete, you're welcome to come back here, if you think you can stand the City again.  We will be glad to see you."

You put the letter back into your pocket. Everything is back to normal. Peter's wedding was amazing. And now you better get some sleep, before you reach Valodsci...

Press a key to continue

You must have slept since you are suddenly awake and completely disoriented. The train stands still and the other people in the carriage have left. Looking out the window you realize that you are back in the city of secrets. But why?

You stand up, you leave the carriage and step down from the train onto solid ground.
