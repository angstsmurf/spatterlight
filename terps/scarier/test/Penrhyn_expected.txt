The Burning Sky

I am Ralph Wyndham of Penrhyn . This is my city.  It's never silent, even in the din of night. Machines always in operation, smog makes things look further away than it is. The purple and red sunsets. Gas and Electricity is the veins and the blood of this city- with commerce and technology coursing through its veins. We live in a bubble literally like those childrens snowglobes only there is no snow for us at least.

Penrhyn has..a history or I should say it is part of history. The short of it is it never used to be like this until a generation or so ago the globe had a huge issue with what the high priest scientists said was the Sun had heated up by five percent. So this lead eventually lead to desertification of the land and deforestation.
It dramatically changed the way of life. To survive cities had to build a high thick glass dome. It had some advantages for some, for us it was fresh vegetables all year round, and no cold winters. The dome itself was designed by the best minds of the world, they had five years to come up with something workable & sustainable to weather the strong angry glare of the Sun. It took six years to build before the Furnace years began."
And that was not the worst of it. For I will tell a story of what lead to Penrhyn's downfall.
