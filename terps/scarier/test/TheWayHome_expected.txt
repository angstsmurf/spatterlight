The Way Home (part 2 of The Bash Saga)

INTRODUCTION

Your name is Bash, a famous warrior, who has just obtained the fabled Dragon Diamond from the Forest of Fear [1]. You are now on your way to deliver the diamond to queen Drana, who ordered you to find the diamond in the first place.

You left the Forest of Fear a few hours ago with the carriage that brought you here. However, the home trip is only for you and the horse as the queen's chauffeur Leon didn't make it [1]. You know you have to go through the desert to get back but first you need to drive to the ice valley in order to have enough water for the return trip.

[1: See the first game "The Dragon Diamond" in The Bash Saga series]

You roll in on the road to the ice valley. Suddenly something hard hits your head and everything becomes black..

You slowly wake up and look around. You find yourself tied to a tree. Your adventure begins here.

South end of garden (tied to a tree)
You are sitting in a snowy garden surrounded by a fence made of solid ice. Several ice statues are scattered around the garden. To the east is the wall of a large ice cabin. You are currently tied to a tree with a rope. You have managed to get the lower part of your left arm free enough to move. In the northern part of the garden, two ice trolls sit around a fire. Two vicious looking dogs sit beside them. Right next to the trolls stands your carriage.  A meter or so away from you lies a sharp stone on the ground.

> ask trolls for help
You talk to the trolls and one of them comes over to you. "So you are awake huh? I guess we have to thank you for bringing us the Dragon Diamond - ha ha. Your quest was rumored around the country, so we just had to wait for you to come by. Tomorrow we will be gone in your carriage with the diamond." The troll grins and goes back to his troll buddy.

> ask trolls for mercy
"Shut up!" shouts one of the trolls. The other troll whispers: "He might be freezing! He hasn't got fur like we have". "Well I do not see him SHAKE so I am sure he is allright." says the first troll.

> shake
You shake to emphasize that you are freezing. The ice trolls see it. "Look, he IS shaking" says one of the trolls and points in your direction. "Okay, okay, I will get him a blanket then" says the other troll. He wanders around the cabin and soon returns with a blanket. The troll walks up to you and says: "I see you got one of your arms free. However, it is not gonna help you untie the hard knots on the back of the tree. Here, help yourself." He drops the blanket over your knees where you can reach it with your one arm. You grab hold of the blanket with your free hand.

> get stone with blanket
By repeatedly throwing the blanket at the stone and carefully dragging the blanket along the ground, you manage to bring the stone close enough to your free arm so that you can pick it up with your hand. You now have the sharp stone.

> cut rope with stone
With the sharp stone, you slowly cut your way through the rope, freeing first your right arm, then your entire body. As the trolls look away you sneak your way out of the garden to the south. Once out, you run west to make sure you are not caught again.
