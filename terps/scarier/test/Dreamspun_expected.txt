Dreamspun

Palace of Dreams

You have built yourself a palace on the lunar surface. Each dream you acquired so long ago, either offered or taken, was a shining pearl to heap up about you. In time you learned to do more than just eat and drink; you wound the mortals’ gossamer thoughts onto spools and arranged them by color, embroidering from these your own creations.

Alas, dreams are much harder to come by now. The Prince, his servants, his children; those who made the dreams you were offered, and those who sent you out to gather the secrets of others. All fleeting things, all gone.

Because none believe in you any longer, you can’t be invited to their world anymore. But every so often, a stray dream comes fluttering up, and you form it into a pearl and hold it fast. In this way you have sustained yourself for centuries...

There is little here for you now.

You sit upon your throne, alone as you have ever been, and dream your own dream of escape.

Here is your dining table, bereft of food. Here is your work bench with spinning wheel and loom, abandoned.

When you gaze out the window across the barren lunar plain, you can see a bountiful green world hanging in the glittering sky.

There must be a way to reach it from your barren prison.

A nacreous reticule is here, awaiting fresh dreams. It holds nothing.

Years pass. The dreams are so infrequent that you make no progress on your rope. Do people down there just not have a sense of wonder anymore?

Famished and restless, you stalk the halls of your palace-turned-prison. Solitude and boredom have you climbing the walls.

You pause at a window and cast a forlorn glance up at the blue-green globe suspended in the velvety black of the night...

It’s then that you see them begin to slowly emerge: a billowing stream of newly minted dreams flowing through space, directly towards the moon. Like the confetti of a thousand parades; like an emperor’s garden of butterflies cast toward your nets.

In a few rotations, the first begin to arrive. Your heart is bursting with joy as you gather in the bounty. It’s not just that this plenty will satisfy your immediate needs, but that you know this may be the chance you’ve waited for. Out of these dreams and those in the remains of brilliant stream that still follows, you’ll weave your lifeline to the mortal world. Something must have happened down there, to turn imaginations, hopes, and a sense of mystery to the moon. It’s true these are not the bonfire blazing visions of the Prince and the rulers and warlords of his day, but many smaller thoughts fluttering in all at once. But so, so many. You may not ever have a chance like this again.

For a short time, dreams will arrive steadily. If the stream dries up before you complete your escape, all will be lost.

> climb down to earth

The stars twinkle by as you slip down the thin filament, transitioning from the icy cold of the void to a welcome heat as you plunge through the Earth's atmosphere, trailing silver sparks. Checking yourself just above a pillowy expanse of clouds, flooded with moonlight, you take a moment to regain your composure.

With each of your eight long, slender limbs trembling with anticipation, you pick your way further down the rope, breaking through the cloudmist to fix your faceted eyes upon the city below...

You have such power here! It crackles through the sky all around you.

"SLEEP," you command the tiny ones below.

"SLEEP."

There are so many bright lights, and bright lives rushing to and fro beneath you. One by one, they dim and stop moving at your urging. Eventually even the machines settle down, rolling to a stop or plunging from the clouds as you did. Peace and silence roll like fog over the city, here and there interspersed with crackling fire.
