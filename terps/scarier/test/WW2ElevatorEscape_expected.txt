WWII Elevator Escape (v3)

Prologue
The German Resistance consisted of small, isolated groups that were unable to mobilize widespread political opposition. Still, successful disclosure of information regarding Nazi armaments factories to the Allies occurred.

You are such a brave soul and you have managed to come very close to Hitler's inner circle. During a meeting in Berlin, Spring 1942, you will have the opportunity to get some crucial information. The meeting is held on the ground floor of a nine-storey building.

However, it is believed that your secret affiliation has just been compromised and that this news will soon reach Hitler and his most trusted men around him. But the meeting is too important to skip.

Thus, the German Resistance has an escape plan for you: As soon as you have received the much needed information, you will leave the meeting room. However, the front door is heavily guarded so you will have to take the elevator to the roof, where a parachute has been hidden. You must then parachute down to the nearby park where the Resistance will have a car waiting for you...

So here you are at the meeting, and you have just received the crucial information that the Allies need. You stand up, put your right hand on your stomach as if you are sick and say: "Sorry! I will be back in a minute.." Very quickly you walk out the door and as you do so, you hear Hitler's voice: "Stop him! He must not leave the building!".

You jump into the elevator and hit the button for the top floor. A guard almost manages to reach the elevator before the door is fully closed and the elevator starts moving. Luckily, this elevator is too fast for the guards to catch up. Let's just hope that no one upstairs pushes the call button...
