The Call of the Shaman

A Note For The Player:

This adventure game is very much "old school" in that the gameplay involves having to SEARCH objects and/or LOOK BEHIND, IN or UNDER objects to find objects that you will need during the game. You might also have to EXAMINE WALL to find something on it.

If you do not like this style of adventure gameplay then regrettably this game may not be for you.

Many thanks

Larry Horsfield

Start Options:

If you have already started the game and wish to restart from a saved position, click on the blue folder icon above.

To Begin the game from the first location, press B and then Enter.

To read the prologue and playing instructions (highly recommended if you  are starting this game for the first time) press P and then Enter.

PLEASE NOTE: This game was envisioned by the author as being playable Cyan On Black. Since ADRIFT does not support an authorial override of a player's background colour, be advised that if your background colour is not black, or your text colour not set to cyan, portions of this game will not be easily readable. For maximum experience as intended by the author please ensure your background colour is set to BLACK and the output colour to CYAN.

If you are visually impaired you can increase or decrease the font size whilst playing this game by holding down the CTRL key and moving your mouse wheel.

Please ensure that Autocomplete is unticked in the "Edit" tab  above.

"Don't worry," you tell her. "The wound is healing nicely," and you proceed to tell you of your quest to find the antidote. Her eyes grow rounder as you tell her of your adventures on the continent of Americanus and how Grey wolf made the antidote to the poison from rattlesnake venom and the silverthorn berries.

You talk for a while and then Bronwynn realises that the marriage ceremony hadn't finished when she was attacked by Prince Timothy and that you still weren't married.

"Well," she says to you in a determined voice. "We'll just have to do something about that!"

Three weeks later, when Bronwynn has completely recovered from her ordeal and is strong enough, the two of you are married without incident in Domreil Cathedral.

And, as the saying goes, you all live happily ever after!
