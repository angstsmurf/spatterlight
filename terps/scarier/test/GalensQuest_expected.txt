Galen's Quest
 Your master, the wizard Galen, has gone missing. You had no clue to his whereabouts, and you drifted off to sleep, wondering where you could begin your search for your master. He might, after all, be in trouble!

Your dreams that night are vivid and disturbing. You see glimpses of your master in strange landscapes among even stranger beings. You have a terrible sensation of movement over vast distances.

You awaken in a world gone mad. No longer in your master's home, you find yourself in a massive courtyard. Blood cascades from the top of an archway before you, and darkness is all around. Could this be where your master has gone? You will have to use your wits to find your way out of this situation, and to hopefully find out what happened to Galen.
