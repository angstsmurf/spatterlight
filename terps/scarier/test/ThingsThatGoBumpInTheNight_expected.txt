Things That Go Bump In The Night!

A Note For The Player:

This adventure game is very much "old school" in that the gameplay involves having to SEARCH objects and/or LOOK BEHIND, IN or UNDER objects to find objects that you will need during the game. You might also have to EXAMINE WALL to find something on it. There is also the possibility of sudden death.

If you do not like this style of adventure gameplay then regrettably this game may not be for you.

I do hope, however, that you will give it a try! A full walkthrough is available on request (type HELP).

Many thanks.

Larry Horsfield

Start Options:

If you have already started the game and wish to restart from a saved position, click on the blue folder icon above.

To read the Prologue, type P and press Enter.

To Begin the game from the first location, type B and then Enter.

You are highly advised to read the playing instructions, especially if you are starting this game for the first time. To do this, when you are playing the game type INST and then Enter.

PLEASE NOTE: This game was envisioned by the author as being playable Cyan On Black. As ADRIFT does not support an authorial override of a player's background colour, be advised that if your background colour is not black, portions of this game will not be easily readable. For maximum experience as intended by the author please ensure your background colour is set to BLACK and the font colour to CYAN. You can adjust these colours by clicking on VIEW in the toolbar above and selecting "Options"
