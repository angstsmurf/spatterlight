PROLOGUE
You are nine years old and you are visiting your Grandpa's Ranch. It's morning and you are playing in the field behind the ranch when a flying saucer suddenly lands on the grass a few meters from you. The door opens and an old woman walks down the stairs. She walks towards you.

What is your name?

> Claude

Inside the Flying Saucer
The circular spaceship is pretty cramped but otherwise quite cosy as Grandma has hung paintings and trinkets on the wall.

You can go:
 - Nowhere until the door is opened

You can see:
- A control panel
- A big viewscreen
- A door
- Grandma

(the compass)

TUTORIAL: Now try to examine the compass: EXAMINE COMPASS (or just: X COMPASS)

> x it
(the compass)
This is the compass Grandpa gave you. You remember Grandpa telling you that on a map, north is always at the top, south is down, east is to the right and west is to the left.

You feel the saucer shake slightly, then Grandma says "We have just landed! Claude, see if you can open the door."

TUTORIAL: This is your first puzzle. First step is always to examine(X) the relevant objects, i.e. X DOOR

> x door

TUTORIAL: Notice the button. Try PUSH GREEN BUTTON (or simply PUSH BUTTON if you haven't found any other buttons)

> press button
You press the green button and the door opens. You can now go OUT.

One point for opening the door.
