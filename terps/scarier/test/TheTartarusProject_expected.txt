"I am you!"

The guest looks exactly like you and claims to be you. "How is this possible?" you ask. Your double looks at you and begins to tell you a long story: "On the one hand this world is real, though not as real as one might imagine. This world is a simulation of physical processes including evolution. Thus your feelings and thoughts are as real as if the world was purely physical. Life is just implemented in a simulation rather than in physical processes.

Approximately 20 years ago, important research goals had been reached with the simulation. For this reason the simulation was cloned, thus 4 parallel simulations of the same universe have been running. I come from such a parallel simulation. These simulations are not 100% identical since the whole point of doing the parallel simulations is to reach different research goals. Thus subtle differences have been included in the parallel simulations to trigger variations between the simulations. However, the research is not progressing as intended and thus the creators of our simulations are considering shutting down the simulations. In other words, we will die if this happens.

Roughly speaking it is a matter of resources in the world of our creators. Already one parallel simulation has been shut down. The survival of a parallel simulation requires success of the Tartarus project, which is currently running in the existing three simulations. The fourth simulation was turned off after the Tartarus project was abandoned. I know this since I have been contacted by the creators of our simulated worlds. The Tartarus project is most significantly progressing in the simulation where I come from. It is also via the Tartarus project I have been able to come to your simulated world. However, I would not have been able to make contact to your "dimension" unless the creator had provided me with some clues on how to modify the Tartarus project in order to travel between simulated realities. Well, now I am here. My mission is to make the Tartarus project succeed in all 3 worlds so that no more worlds need to be destroyed.

For the sake of ease, let us call the parallel simulations 'worlds'. My world will be safe if I can make it home to my world again, since travel has then been established between worlds, which is the objective of the Tartarus project seen with the eyes of our Creators. Similarly, your world will be safe, since travel has then also been established from here. Thus first task must be to get me home. Then at least two worlds are safe."

It takes some minutes for you to truly believe what you just have been told. On the one hand, this story is too amazing. On the other hand the proof sits right in front of you, and you always had an open mind when it came to the simulation argument, i.e. the theory stating that IF such powerful simulations can be achieved, we are most likely living inside of one. In addition, your knowledge of the Tartarus project tells you that traveling between simulations might be possible. You talk for a while about the specifics of the Tartarus project which simply confirms that your double knows all there is to know about the project. You are convinced.

"So how do we get you back to your own world then?" you ask. Your double answers: "You need to update the core code of the Tartarus Simulator. Then I sit down in the Simulation Chair and you type in the coordinates I give you. Finally you activate the simulator and I should be transferred to my world". He hands you a USB-key with the core code and a note with the coordinates.

"As easy as this may sound we are going to have trouble getting past security" you say. "Meet me outside Fu-tech tonight 7:00pm and we will give it a try. I need to go back to the university and perform my daily tasks, so that no-one becomes suspicious." With those words you stand up and leave your double...
