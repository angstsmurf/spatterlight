The deserted street.

You are standing outside a huge, quite impressive, iron gate. To the north, through the gate, lies a garden and an old abandoned house, silhouetted against the pale silvery light from the full moon. The street that led you here runs south, back towards the town you came from. Ahead of you the street bends towards the west and leads towards the dark mountains on the horizon. Across the street, to the east, empty fields stretch out towards the horizon.

The nearest lamp post is far away, the only light coming from the moon hanging silently in the dark sky high above your head. Through the rails of the gate you can see a graveled path leading through a garden up towards the old abandoned house.
