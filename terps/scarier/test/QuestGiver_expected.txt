Please type rules to learn how to play the game.

This is an adventure game with a twist. You are the Quest Giver.

Basic overview:
You can have up to 3 Quest cards at at time and only 1 quest per Adventurer. Hiring Adventurers costs a commission and they will try to complete the quest over time. You cannot hire adventurers at night, sleep at a tavern to pass the night away.

Note: The game will end in 10 days time (not literally) (40 turns = 1 day. 20 for daylight and 20 for night time)

It can take many turns to reach a destination. Also at the moment if you head in a direction to travel to another village you cannot go back. I will fix this in future.

Enter draw a quest to draw 1 card. Repeat until you have 3 Quest cards. Then type view prospects

Good luck..

Home Village
This village isn't really your home but you know more people here than elsewhere. The food is great and the village is a trading hub that a lot of travelers pass through.

You see a sign that tells you where the Tavern is, which is across the street.

Adventurer 1, Adventurer 2, Adventurer 3 and Adventurer 4 are here.
Exits are north and west.

> rules
Main goal:

You have 10 days to complete the game. Try to accumulate as many successful quests and money earned from them. As well as accumulate as much high Quest value scores as well. All of these will be totaled at the end for your final grand score.

You must go to each village and find adventurers to hire and give a quest to. Quests have two main attributes. The first being the most important. Each will be rolled against the adventurer. So luck can play a part. But try and match the adventurers attributes score as close as you can for better chance of success.

Some quests are common and some rare, which might unlock new quests and rewards.

Quests:

You can only have up to 3 Quest cards at a time (held). You can only give one quest to one adventurer at a time.

How well you match the adventurer to the quest will go in favor of it succeeding but sometimes things go wrong. Each quest has a quest value which is a score value that accumulates towards your total score at the end. Gold coins you gain from completion. But you need to spend gold coins to hire Adventurers and also sleep at a tavern. You cannot hire adventurers at night.

You cannot drop quest cards you must get rid of them by give quest command when at a village where adventurers are.

Villages
Where you find adventurers to send them on quests and gain new cards if you are able to take some more.

Enter Commands for all the main commands in the game.

> commands
Commands:

rules  (how to play the game)
draw card  (draw limit is 3)
view cards (Quest cards you have in your hand)
view prospects (Only view available adventurers stats when at a village. Alt command: view pro)
view hired  (View Adventures you have already hired and what Quest they have)
give a quest (choose a quest to give to an adventurer)
view hired (view list of hired adventurers on a quest)
east  (or E for short to move east or other directions n,s,ne,se,w etc)
enter tavern  (Then talk to the barman for a room to sleep the night)
talk to barman  (then ask barman about a room or rumors. Or instead ask about topic,eg a room)
inventory  (list what you are holding and stats)
enter tavern  (list what you are holding and stats)
sleep  (When you have hired a room from the barman and are inside the tavern)
wait  (pass time using up 3 turns)

Best way to play is when arriving at a new village, view cards you have and then view prospects to work out if any are worth hiring before using the give quest command if there is a suitable match.
