   The Mysterious Spaceship

Ouch! Not again! Your one-man spaceship is out of fuel and this time it is more critical than ever. You are orbiting the planet Karpath and you are about to run out of oxygen too. Your only chance of survival is to reach another spaceship, which is also orbiting Karpath.

You leave your spaceship and float in your spacesuit through the empty space towards the foreign spaceship. The last thing you see before you float into the airlock is the moon of Karpath. The airlock closes just in time, as you run out of oxygen. Luckily there is oxygen aboard the ship. If you can get this spaceship started, you can land on Karpath.
