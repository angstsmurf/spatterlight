 Intro

Captain Pearson is a Salvager through and through and always gets the job done. The Captain owes a favor to an old friend, a friend who saved his life many years ago and now needs help. A freighter belonging to the company of his friend has been missing for a good couple of months, its transponder had turned off midway though its journey. He wishes to know what happened to it and retrieve its black box if possible. He has given Captain Pearson a ship The Avadora to go find it and he has 500 turns in which to achieve that goal.
