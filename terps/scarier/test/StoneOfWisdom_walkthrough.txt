# A real, linear, max-score playthrough: 50 out of 50 points, *** You have won ***

# Derived by playing the game; the bundled ROT13 hint sheet supplied the puzzle
# solutions, the connecting room-to-room navigation was solved from the map.
#
# Puzzle spine:
#   * Light the lamp, descend into the abandoned mine.
#   * Knock the troll UNCONSCIOUS with the combat ring (4x "hit troll with ring";
#     NEVER kill it -- killing reduces karma and the creatures' village gate later
#     only admits good karma, making the game unwinnable). Search it for the key.
#   * Grab the rope at the dead end; unlock the Manager's office with the skeleton
#     key; lock yourself in, eat and sleep; tie the rope to the table, throw it out
#     of the window and climb down to the small hole; cross the bamboo bridge
#     (it collapses -> you land in the net in the inner-mountain forest).
#   * Dwarf village: distract Milla (say yes) and free Puk from the pillory.
#   * Honey the food rations in the beehive up the clearing tree.
#   * Creatures' village (across the river, karma-gated): Puk gives a silver coin
#     and the secret-brick coordinates; Bumil confirms the mission; sell the sword
#     to Pamba for a gold coin.
#   * Behind the dwarf castle: examine + push the two marked bricks to open the
#     secret door; in the cellar, sleep the guard dog with a honeyed ration and
#     take the Stone of Wisdom from the pedestal.
#   * Show the stone to Bumil, buy Luma's canoe (1 gold + 1 silver), carry it to
#     the river and SAIL IN CANOE -> the river drags you home -> win.
#
