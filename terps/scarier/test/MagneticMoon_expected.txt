Magnetic Moon

A Note For The Player:

This adventure game is very much "old school" in that the gameplay involves having to SEARCH objects and/or LOOK BEHIND, IN or UNDER objects to find objects that you will need during the game. You might also have to EXAMINE WALL to find something on it.

If you do not like this style of adventure gameplay then regrettably this game may not be for you.

Many thanks

Larry Horsfield

Start Options:

If you have already started the game and wish to restart from a saved position, click on the blue folder icon above.

To Begin the game from the first location, press B and then Enter.

To read the prologue and playing instructions (highly recommended if you  are starting this game for the first time) press P and then Enter.

PLEASE NOTE: This game was programmed by the author with mostly Cyan text on a Black background. Since ADRIFT does not support an authorial override of a player's background colour, be advised that if your background colour is not BLACK and font colour CYAN, portions of this game will not be easily readable. For maximum experience as intended by the author please ensure your background colour is set to BLACK and the output colour to CYAN.

If you are visually impaired you can increase or decrease the font size whilst playing this game by holding down the CTRL key and moving your mouse wheel.

Please ensure that Autocomplete is unticked in the "Edit" tab above.

The Control Room
You're seated at your console in the control room of the Stellar Queen. There is a wide viewscreen on the forward bulkhead, in front of which is the helmsman's console, with the Captain's command chair behind that. Arranged around the rear of the room, either side of you, are the consoles that control other ship's functions, in between which, leading Aft, are the exit doors. The noise made by the repair crew is very loud and it is a veritable assault on your eardrums.  Commander Roffey, Commander Ringshaw, Captain Rosenloev and the repairmen are here.

> wait
You wait impatiently for a while.

Eventually, the captain stands up from his command chair, reaches his arms up above his head and groans as he stretches the kinks out of his body. He turns to Cmdr.Roffey and shouts,

"OK Tony, you have the conn, I'd better go and join the away team".

The repairmen realise that the captain has got to his feet and they all stop work in respect, giving you a bit of respite from the noise. Cmdr.Roffey gets up from his console and takes the captain's place in the command chair as you all get to your feet.

"Very well, Sir, I have the conn." he tells the captain.

The captain looks around the room at you all and sketches a quick salute.

"Carry on, ladies and gentlemen," he says as he heads for the exit doors, dodging the various repairmen, a couple of whom have to step back to let him pass.

The doors judder closed after him and the noise returns as everyone resumes what they were doing, except yourself of course as you haven't got anything to do at the moment.
