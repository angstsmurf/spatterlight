The Spectre of Castle Coris

A Note For The Player:

This adventure game is very much "old school" in that the gameplay involves having to SEARCH objects and/or LOOK BEHIND, IN or UNDER objects to find objects that you will need during the game. You might also have to EXAMINE WALL to find something on it.

If you do not like this style of adventure gameplay then regrettably this game might not be for you.

Many thanks

Larry Horsfield

Please type the letter O then press Enter to Continue to the Start Options Page.

THE SPECTRE OF CASTLE CORIS - Start Options:

Press B (enter) to Begin the Adventure from the First Location.

Press P (enter) to read the Prologue and Playing Instructions.
(Recommended if starting this adventure for the first time)

If you have already started the adventure and wish to restart from a saved position, click on the Blue Folder Icon in the taskbar above.

PLEASE NOTE: This game was envisioned by the author as being playable Cyan On Black. Since ADRIFT does not support an authorial override of a player's background colour, be advised that if your background colour is not black, portions of this game will not be easily readable. For maximum experience as intended by the author please ensure your background colour is set to BLACK and the output colour to CYAN.

If you are visually impaired you can increase or decrease the font size whilst playing this game by holding down the CTRL key and moving your mouse wheel.

Please ensure that Autocomplete is unticked in the "Edit" tab  above.

As you walk towards Corwyn, you meet some men, women & children, laden with what appears to be all of their worldly goods, heading east. You stop to speak with them, and you realise that they are very frightened.

"If you've any sense, you'll turn around and go straight back to where you've just come from!" one of the men tells you. "We've decided to leave before the Spectre visits us!" a young woman calls out as she hurries past.

You try to ask them about this 'spectre', but they are too set on getting as much distance between themselves and the town as they hurry east along the road. You head west towards Corwyn, determined to get to the bottom of this 'spectre' business.

Corwyn Town Square
You're in the main square of the small town of Corwyn. The damp, rubbish-strewn cobblestones are empty of life, and a stone war memorial stands forlornly in the middle. Surrounding the square are houses and shops, most of which are empty or boarded up. The wide road leads East and continues West from the square. Narrower streets wind off North and South.

As you stand looking around at the deserted town square, you feel the crawling sensation in your back that warns you that somebody is watching you.

Sure enough, you see a man staring at you from the building at the corner of the street that leads north from the square. When he sees that you have spotted him, the man turns and walks away along the street. You see him enter a building further along the street on the west side.
