Grandpa's Ranch

You are nine years old and you are visiting your Grandpa's Ranch. Grandpa says that he will rather give his family some of his valuables while he is still alive. However, he wants you to earn it and so you are here on some sort of treasure hunt, devised by your Grandpa. Exciting!

* If you cannot see the map, press CTRL and M or click on "Window" followed by "Show map".

* Move graphics to separate window (recommended): CTRL+G. If you don't, the text scrolls less smoothly. Type LAYOUT for more information.

* If you need a break, you can SAVE your game and RESTORE it later.

* Please no more than two words (except the word "the" which is ignored anyway)

* Examples:
 - GO NORTH (or just N)  - OPEN DOOR
 - TALK GRANDPA          - UNLOCK CAR
 - INVENTORY (or just I) - LOOK

* Special commands: ABOUT - HELP - WORDS - LAYOUT - CREDITS - UNDO - QUIT - RESTART - MUSIC ON/OFF - TUTORIAL ON/OFF
