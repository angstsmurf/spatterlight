Veteran Experience
Written by Robert Rafgon

Tonight is going to be the biggest night in your long, long professional
wrestling career. Your name is the Veteran and you have been entered into a
four wrestler World title tournament. Maybe the World title is only
recognised in this small town in the middle of nowhere but it it is a World
title nevertheless. You have never won a World title but you know it is your
destiny. It will be your reward for years of being hated by crowds who
obviously did not understand you.

You were not originally scheduled in the tournament. In fact you were not
scheduled on the show at all but a last minute unfortunate car accident to
the Brawler left an opening. With all the other matches set up already, you
were a late call-up to the tournament and will face the Youth, the High Flyer
and the Monster. Physically you are much older and weaker so you cannot match
them. However, you know you are smarter and plan to take our your opponents
before the matches even begin.

Your locker room
This room is empty apart from a bench along one wall. You can only head
north.

Also here is a bag.

>A normal bench.

>Your bag that you brought in for tonight.  The bag is closed.

>[take bag]
You pick up the bag.

>[open bag]
You open the bag.  A crowbar is inside the bag.

>A crowbar

>You take the crowbar from the bag.

>You move north.
Eastern corridor
This corridor extends to the west. To the south is your locker room. To the
north is the Youth's locker room.

>You move north.
The Youth's locker room
The Youth is preening himself in front of a mirror. Near him are the gifts
from some female admirers. It is a long time since you had any females after
you. In fact you don't think they ever were. They all preferred the Star and
for that the Youth is going to pay. You can only head south.

Also here is a bunch of flowers and a teddy bear.

The Youth is here.

>A second generation wrestler who is the handsome son of the Star, a former
World champion. He is the most popular wrestler right now and the favourite
for the tournament tonight. You really want to win the tournament yourself
but if you cannot manage it you would be happy just to take the Youth out.
His father thwarted you from fame and fortune too many times to ever be
forgiven.

>This is a full-length mirror, specially brought in so the Youth can look his
best. It is sickening to see a wrestler making himself pretty. Wrestling is
supposed to be rough and brutal.

>These flowers smell so nice you almost want to throw up.

>A teddy bear given to the Youth.

>You pick up the teddy bear.

>You move south.
Eastern corridor
This corridor extends to the west. To the south is your locker room. To the
north is the Youth's locker room.

>You move west.
Central corridor
This corridor extends to locker rooms in the east and west. To the south is
the ring.

>You move south.
The Ring
The ring has been set up earlier and is surrounded by empty chairs, waiting
for the fans to come in for the big show later in the night. You can only
head north.

>These chairs are empty.

>This is where the action will occur tonight and you will finally achieve your
destiny. This ring has been the home to many epic matches over the years,
including your heartbreaking World title defeat to the Star, the father of
the current wrestler the Youth. No matter how many weapons you pulled out
from under the ring and used behind the referee's back you could not defeat
him.

>You see a bag of tacks and a ladder under the ring.

>A bag filled with tacks.

>You pick up the bag of tacks.

>A ladder.

>You pick up the ladder.

>You move north.
Central corridor
This corridor extends to locker rooms in the east and west. To the south is
the ring.

>You move west.
Western corridor
This corridor extends to the east. To the north is the Monster's locker room.
To the south is the High Flyer's locker room.

>You move north.
The Monster's locker room
The Monster is here, sitting on the floor brooding, in preparation for his
title challenge. The light is broken, leaving the room near-dark apart from a
few candles on the floor. You can only head south.

The Monster is here.

>The Monster is a large fearsome beast who all the other wrestlers are wary
of. He is unpredictable and the only wrestler in the federation who is hated
by the fans more than you. The Monster has demolished you previously when you
tried to interfere in one of his matches and you would be almost scared of
him, but you are scared of no one. You think he must have a soft side because
you once saw him being nice to some kids. Of course this could just be his
childlike temperament.  The Monster is carrying a bottle of acid.

>You see no such thing.

>These are the only light source in the room.

>The Monster grabs the teddy bear from you and hugs it to himself. Maybe he
does have a soft side. However, by the way he bites into the bear and starts
to tear it apart maybe not. Well, he is at least distracted now.

>You take advantage of the distraction to hit the Monster over the head with
the crowbar and knock him out. Now he cannot make the tournament so you have
eliminated an opponent.

>This is a bottle of acid.

>You take the bottle of acid from the unconscious Monster.

>You move south.
Western corridor
This corridor extends to the east. To the north is the Monster's locker room.
To the south is the High Flyer's locker room.

>You move south.
The High Flyer's locker room
The High Flyer is here practicing his aerial maneuvers before the tournament
on a mattress on the floor. You don't see that there is a place in wrestling
for these flashy stupid moves and would gladly outlaw them. You can only head
north.

The High Flyer is here.

>The High Flyer is very popular and known for his tremendous jumping ability,
able to execute a Shooting Star Press, Moonsault, Frog Splash or whatever
else is possible from the top turnbuckle. No one has ever seen his face, as
he wears a mask with a jet on it at all times. It is rumoured that the day
the High Flyer is unmasked is the day he will retire.  The High Flyer is
carrying a jet mask.

>This is the jet mask worn by the High Flyer.

>A mattress lies in the middle of the room.

>The High Flyer thanks you because he can now practice his moves from higher
up. He immediately begins to jump from the ladder.

>The High Flyer lands on the tacks and screams out in agony as they tear into
him. Luckily no one else notices. It does not look like he will be moving
anywhere soon but he may recover in time for the matches tonight.

>You walk over to the High Flyer and remove his mask. He screams out and tries
to stop you but in his weakened state he is no match. You finally see the
High Flyer's face and it is not much better looking than your own. He cries
and tells you he will now have to retire. Oh well, there goes one opponent.

>You put on the jet mask.

>You move north.
Western corridor
This corridor extends to the east. To the north is the Monster's locker room.
To the south is the High Flyer's locker room.

>You move east.
Central corridor
This corridor extends to locker rooms in the east and west. To the south is

>You move east.

The Youth's locker room
The Youth is preening himself in front of a mirror. Near him are the gifts
from some female admirers. It is a long time since you had any females after
you. In fact you don't think they ever were. They all preferred the Star and
for that the Youth is going to pay. You can only head south.

Also here is a bunch of flowers.

The Youth is here.

>The Youth soon recognises you are not the High Flyer but it is still too
late. You take the acid and empty it on the Youth's face. He screams in agony
as the acid burns and destroys his handsome face. That will teach him to be
too proud of his appearance. You wonder if any of his fans will remain in his
new state. The Youth's screams eventually bring help and he is carted off to
hospital.

Later in the night when the tournament is scheduled to begin the promoters
are forced to make the announcement that as there is only one wrestler left
healthy, the winner by default is the Veteran. Congratulations on finally
fulfilling your destiny. No one else believed that you could but you knew
fate would take a hand to help you.

Congratulations!
