The Secret of The Lost World
**********
It was supposed to be a wonderful holiday, a journey around the world. You
packed your things and boarded the plane. After a long traveling you got to a
far-off part of India, where you spent some restful days waiting for a ship
to continue your journey.
After weeks filled with weariness and sickness a strong wind appeared and
then storm and hurricane came. Your ship was left helpless in the fight
against the forces of the nature, just to be cast on the rocks of an island
it was just passing along.
The last thing you remember is a noise of thunderstorm, the passengers'
desperate screams and burning of wood.
