We are coming to get you!

by Richard Otter, version 1.0

It really is so much fun being a germ. Not only do you travel the world from
one flesh-sack to another, you get to meet your millions of relatives and
breed. You have never been that sure what infection you are, but you are
pretty sure it is something really nasty. Yes, you really can't beat being a
germ.

Only this morning you began a new adventure, when your flesh-sack sneezed you
onto the hand of another.  Unfortunately you couldn't get through the skin
and had nearly given up hope. When, your victim got a tiny cut on its hand
poor thing!

Your objective? Infect every location you can find and then escape.

How to infect? Find a weak point and 'infect' away.
