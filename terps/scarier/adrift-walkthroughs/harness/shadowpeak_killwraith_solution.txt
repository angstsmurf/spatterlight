# Variant of the canonical 710/790 win that ALSO:
#   * kills the wraith Alam Nadeer at the sarcophagus (room 119) the moment he
#     appears - 8x `attack alam nadeer` with the Seeker sword (T393 +20); and
#   * kills Fang the wolf in the swamp (room 27, T31 +5, self-syncing attack/z
#     loop because Fang wanders rooms 27-33); and
#   * cuts down the werewolf Margo in the prison (room 98, T255 +5).
# Net: 710 + 20 + 5 + 5 = 740/790. The killing of the wraith shifts the seeded
# combat RNG, so the deep-dungeon Damastus maze chase was re-derived (greedy BFS)
# and the Hell Cerberus fight widened to 8 attacks.
#
# NOTE on the score ceiling: 740 is near the practical maximum for a wraith-kill
# route. The only further points are Woody +10 (T529) and "examine dragon" +10
# (T387). The dragon task is UNREACHABLE - its counter object is permanently
# placed in room 81 and the task requires it hidden, which never happens. Woody
# is a wandering wererat with Agility 60 / Defence 30 who flees after every hit
# and retreats to an off-map lair (room 204); with the player's high-variance
# melee damage the killing blow rarely lands before he escapes (verified across
# several RNG alignments). So >750 while killing the wraith is not achievable in
# practice; the ceiling is ~745 (Woody excluded, the hard 203-stamina graveyard
# zombie-mob battle for Rex's +5 included).
