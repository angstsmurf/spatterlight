Shadow of the Past
"What happened," you ask yourself.  The last thing you remember is delivering
newspapers while riding your bicycle.  You vaguely remember a car swerving
and almost hitting you.  You went flying off the bike and hit the pavement.
As you look around, you realize that you are not only not outside, but you
are lying on a stone floor in some kind of cave, and the coldness is seeping
into your bones making them ache.  How can this be?  You were outside and it
was 87 degrees.  You quickly stand up and start rubbing your hands together
for warmth and realize that something else is wrong.  It's not just the fact
you are in a strange place, but your hands do not appear to be your own.
They are bigger, a man's hands.  How can these be yours?  You're just a ten
year old boy.  You check out the rest of your body.  Yep, you're a  man now.
You have had a great desire to have an adventure and to be the hero.  You
have always felt that something was missing in your life, but this is
ridiculous.  Well, this could be pretty cool anyway.
