The Town of Azra
The Town of Azra is placed in a primitive/Ancient Roman setting. To prosper
in this town, you need to know the basic skills of hunting, then you can
build more skills and gain more money. This game is still under development
and bugs/errors will be found!

You are Pindarus, a semi-tall, semi-muscular man living in the Town of Azra.
You have dark, shoulder-length hair and deep, dark, commanding eyes.

Press any key for more information...

The main goals I have included in this first release are:

1.) Kill a bandit, and get his money
2.) Kill a deer, take the carcass and sell it to Drako
3.) Make a few purchases from Harthro's, Calado's, Golapho's
4.) Stay at Gralle's Inn
5.) Purchase a house
6.) Learn Stealth Tactics

By the way, a few new commands... type 'stats' to take a look at a few stats,
and 'wealth' to just look at the money you have. Also, the money system is
divided into dollars and cents. Whenever you are in a shop, you may
interchange the two amounts (for example. if you had 150 cents, and was in
Harthro's Golden Tavern, you could type 'change cents into a dollar' and you
would then have 50 cents, and one extra dollar, and vice versa.

After you complete those tasks, you're pretty much done with this... I'm not
saying that you can't continue to kill more bandits and sell more carcasses
to gain more money, of course. :)

Press any key to continue...

So there you have it! The game is nowhere near completion. Enjoy what I have
already made... Remember... its only a game. -Stan Tencza, Creator of 'The
Town of Azra'.
