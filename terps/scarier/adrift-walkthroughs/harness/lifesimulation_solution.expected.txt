Life Simulation V 1.3
Welcome Player, to Life Simulation Version 1.3. There is no objective to this
game, just do what ever you want to do and have fun.

* You start off living in an appartment, with only $50 to spend at your
leisure at the various shops around.

* The name of the city you are in is called Hudson, which is a fictional city
but looks kind of the same as New York or some kind of capital city (although
it is in Australia, where I come from).

* You can talk to different people by using the command "chat to (player)".
If the chat is succesful, you will be given a list of topics that you can
talk to them about later on.

* You can die in this world, if you do anything stupid that is, not of old
age.

* There is a time system which tells you what time it is if you look at your
watch. The time goes up in 30 minute blocks, which go up every so often. It
uses 24 hour time. You may need to set appointments between certain times.

* You can go to sleep in your bed by typing sleep in your bedroom. You will
go to sleep for 8 hours and your health will be at max. You can also nap in
this adventure for 1 hour, but your stamina won't be full.

* You lose $50 a day to pay for bills, (which is $350 a week). This includes,
rent, electricity, water etc. If you don't pay your bills then you will go
into minus money, which is kind of like a debt. Every time you recieve money
that is how much you take off your debt, because it goes directly to the
bank. You might not have any money for food.

* To get back into your house once outside, type unlock door.

* It is a good idea to get a credit card, otherwise you cannot access your
account where you get your pay from.
