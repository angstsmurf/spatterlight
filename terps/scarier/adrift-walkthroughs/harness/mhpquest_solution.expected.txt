Copyright 1995, 1996, 2002 by Adam G Crutchlow.
Ported from Inform to ADRIFT, 2002.

Your daughter Crystal has recently become ill, but you do not know why. It is
obvious to you that if she isn't cured soon she will die. You have heard
stories about a magical plant which grows somewhere around this region, but
you have never actually seen it. It is said to be able to cure any illness.
You must set off to find it. It is your only chance to save your daughter.
Good luck!
