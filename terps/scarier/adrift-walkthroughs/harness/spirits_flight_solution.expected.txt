The Spirit's Flight
An easy game with a background into the Mystical belief. The only hard bit is
remembering all the NPC's names.

All has been peaceful, at least since your arrival into the spirit world.

However, one day, all went wrong. You and the spirits could feel that life
and death was starting to join. They would join because the elementals were
missing from The Stone Circle. These elements of water, fire, air, and earth
along with the spirits kept life and death separate.

If the elements were not returned to the Stone Circle, the centre of the
world, life would not exist and neither would the spirits.

The removal of the elementals was disastrous but this continued with the
separation of the four. This act caused the world to split into four, each
part corresponding to each elemental. Only the Glade of Spirits remained
intact. However, there was also the regeneration of evil beings past
vanquished. It's best that you destroy them if they cause any harm.

You decide that enough was enough. You convinced the Spirit God to change you
from a spirit back into a living soul so that you can retrieve the elementals
and return them to the Stone Circle where they belong.
