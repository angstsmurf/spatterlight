a bored game
by

Ken Franklin

. . . .  Press a key to continue

For some strange reason best known to the author, you find yourself standing
on the first square of a snakes and ladders board carrying just a dice.

You are playing a game of snakes and ladders, so it is basically a game of
chance. To play you just type roll dice and the player advances by one to six
spaces. A degree of control is given because you can roll high or roll low,
this restricts the throw to 4-6 or 1-3 respectively. (These can be abreviated
to R, H and L for speed)

At any time you can type board to see an image of the board that is used in
the game. You may wish to change the graphics display to use the independent
graphics wiindow rather than inline graphics.
