Three levels of play difficulty are available in David Good's "Menagerie!":
Easy, Medium, Hard.  Medium is the default setting.  To change, you must type
Easy or Hard before any scoring.  Once you have selected a difficulty level,
you cannot select another level without restarting the game.

Introduction

You're Willow Murphy, card-carrying member of Greenpeace, Save the Whales,
and the Sea Turtle Preservation Society.  Your current membership in PETA
(People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals) has brought you to the Waleri
Bros. Menagerie and Circus.  Your assignment - capture the unethical
treatment of animals on your trusty video camera.  But you better not get
caught!  They say those elephant handlers are real animals!
