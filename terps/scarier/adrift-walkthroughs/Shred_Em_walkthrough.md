# Shred 'Em — walkthrough

- **Engine:** ADRIFT 4 (native). David Good (as "DuoDave"), ADRIFT 4th 1-Hour
  Comp 2004. Destroy the incriminating files before the SEC arrives.
- **Result:** **WIN** — ends `Due to lack of evidence, the SEC is unable to
  prosecute your boss. She gives you a promotion, and a raise!` Solution:
  `harness/shred_em_solution.txt`; golden `shred_em_solution.expected.txt`;
  marker `Due to lack of evidence`.
- **Walkthrough source:** *Key & Compass* (native-ADRIFT).

## Derivation

Trivial and runs straight through. The view/desk/telephone aren't real objects;
the only mechanic is `turn on shredder` then `take files` / `shred them`
**five times**. The fifth shred scores +50 and triggers the winning ending.
